Final deliverable contains following:
- Complete Application
- Final Report
- Final Presentation

The details of each deliverable is already provided in "Details" folder.


To submit final deliverable you shall put the above mentioned things inside
a single folder then compress it with RAR or ZIP format, and submit the compressed file
on VULMS within due date. Remember there is no extended day in any project deliverable.
Usually VULMS accepted any file less than 100 MB, but if your file is large
or you are having any error on VULMS on uploading the file, then you shall upload your
final deliverable on Google Drive or Dropbox and send the sharing link to your
project supervisor, within due date. No submission will be considered after the due date.
